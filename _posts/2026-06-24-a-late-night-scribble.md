And what if one day  
You and I  
On a winter’s night  
In a street framed softly  
by the yellow of the street lights  


The world seeming to narrow  
Precisely to this very moment,  
The silence weaved thick  
Against the words solemn  


Would my heart still flutter ?  
Would my words still fumble ?  
As if they were meant to hurt me yet heal the same time ?  
A story  in-waiting, for us to be rewritten ?  


You and I, we could spark  
But darling I got this etched a long ago,  
Not all fires were meant to burn me slowly in the dark
