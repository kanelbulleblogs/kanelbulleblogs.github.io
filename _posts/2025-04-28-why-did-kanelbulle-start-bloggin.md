So why did Kanelbulle start blogging, you ask ?



Before I answer this question, let’s take a step back and answer why the name - Kanelbulle ?

The first year I spent here in Sweden, for a long (*like really long* ) time I grappled with understanding the obsession of Swedes for kanelbulle (*literal translation from Swedish- cinnamon balls ; eng - cinnamon buns* ). And it wasn’t just the cinnamon balls, there were balls of all kind :nerd_face: - saffranbulle, kardemumbulle, vaniljbulle … you name it and we had them here, not to forget the meat balls.



Anyway, so to say the kanelbulle stood out amongst them all. 



If something lit up a room, an aura more desired than norrsken[^1], was a person walking into a room with a basket full of freshly baked kanelbullar and everyone would go crazy !



I used to dread the fikas [^2], not another kanelbulle tossed my way. And then there were time it felt being taken hostage- picture being tied up with a chain of kanelbullar [^3], and if you want to indulge your imagination further - may be one stuffed in my mouth (classic Bollywood style). 



And as you would have it, a classic case of Stockholm Syndrome (the irony) , I became the very thing I once feared, I fell for them (literally and figuratively both) . 



I now eat kanelbullar like they are MnMs , with no guilt whatsoever. 



Sad ?  
___Kanelbulle___  



Happy ?  
___Kanelbulle___  

No particular reason ?  
___Kanelbulle___   



At this point, I wouldn’t be surprised if I name my kid kanelbulle.



Decided to blog ?  
___Hell yeah , why not kanelbulle ?___  



I often think about this quote from this Bollywood movie I watched - The Lunchbox 



___“You forget things if you don’t have anyone to tell them to”___



<mark>And it resonated .</mark> 



I never thought I could pick up a pen, not counting the times I aggressively played this completely random game we called “pen-fight” on our school desk tops. 



And if I meet this kid slacking off their schoolwork to go play cricket, eat shaved ice, pick fights and hide their grandma’s cane, some twenty - twenty five years back in time and tell them - “The future you took writing as a hobby and started a blog” , that kid would blow raspberries at me and call me a dork. 



Anyway , so here we are ! This blog could be my way of remembering things, few years from now when I look back. I can post random nonsense you know ? And that too without being shooed off ? And still save my place at the family table ? 



Sounds like a good deal ! (I could anytime have sworn defying endogamy[^4] would have me eating off plastic plates at the family gatherings but here we are)



 Anyhoo, this is supposed to be my hobby project and I don’t really expect any reach but if you still are here with me, I am glad you found me and I hope you enjoy following through ! I don’t know how much I can keep up with but I will try.
